Hi! My name is Noe Gonzalez

I live in the small town of Stephentown, New York. People consider me an avid learner. My different studies have taken me to work as a teacher, office manager, medical interpreter, computer tech, handyman and woodworker.

One of my outstanding traits is that I am a hard working professional eager to help people with their tech/ web development needs.

About Me

I am a web development student, passionate about the tech world and the rapid growth of web development. Throughout this past year, I have noticed the need for more web development solutions to our everyday life situation. As a former computer lab teacher I believe that there is no I in the word team, working together we can accomplish so much more.

Coming back to my tech roots have made me eager to learn and share my knowledge and experiences with others.

Other Interests:

During the past couple of years, I have developed other hobbies/ interests as self-care. These include:

  1. Woodcarving
  2. Pyrography
  3. Watercolor Painting
